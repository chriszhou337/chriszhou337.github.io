Cities Skylines Modding

Essential mods for Cities Skylines and Cities Skylines 2

Introduction

Recommended Mods for Cities Skylines 1

  • Road Anarchy: Allows placement of roads and other objects without restrictions.
  • Move It: Allows movement and rotation of objects freely.
  • Network Extensions 2: Adds new road types and other transportation options.
  • TM:PE (Traffic Manager: President Edition): Manage traffic flow and customize road behavior.
  • Find It: Search for assets and mods in the game.

Recommended Mods for Cities Skylines 2

  • Prop Anarchy: Allows placing of props and other objects without restrictions.
  • Building Anarchy: Allows placing of buildings and other structures without restrictions.
  • Advanced Road Tools: Adds new road types and other transportation options.
  • TM:PE 2 (Traffic Manager: President Edition 2): Manage traffic flow and customize road behavior.
  • Find It 2: Search for assets and mods in the game.
