Cities Skylines Modding

Essential mods for Cities Skylines and Cities Skylines 2

Introduction

Cities Skylines is a city-building game developed by Colossal Order and published by Paradox Interactive. This website will provide information on essential mods for both games, as well as tips and tricks for modding.

Recommended Mods for Cities Skylines 1

  • Road Anarchy: Allows you to place roads and other objects without restrictions.
  • Move It: Allows you to move and rotate objects freely.
  • Network Extensions 2: Adds new road types and other transportation options.
  • TM:PE (Traffic Manager: President Edition): Allows you to manage traffic flow and customize road behavior.
  • Find It: Allows you to search for assets and mods in the game.

Recommended Mods for Cities Skylines 2

  • Prop Anarchy: Allows you to place props and other objects without restrictions.
  • Building Anarchy: Allows you to place buildings and other structures without restrictions.
  • Advanced Road Tools: Adds new road types and other transportation options.
  • TM:PE 2 (Traffic Manager: President Edition 2): Manage traffic flow and customize road behavior.
  • Find It 2: Search for assets and mods in the game.
